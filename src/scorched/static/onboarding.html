Your daily cycle
8:30 AM ET
Phase 1: Morning Analysis
Claude analyzes market data, technicals, macro indicators, and news. Generates trade recommendations with full reasoning.
9:30 AM ET
Phase 1.5: Circuit Breaker
Safety gate checks each buy recommendation against real-time conditions -- gap-downs, price drift, VIX spikes. Blocks risky entries.
9:35 AM ET
Phase 2: Trade Execution
Approved recommendations are executed via your broker (or recorded in paper mode). Fills are logged to the database.
4:01 PM ET
Phase 3: End-of-Day Review
Claude reviews all open positions, suggests stop adjustments, and updates the strategy playbook with lessons learned.
